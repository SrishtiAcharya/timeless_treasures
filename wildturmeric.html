Curcuma Aromatica Or Wild Turmeric



Curcuma Aromatica Or Wild turmeric


By Admin
July 20, 2022


Curcuma Aromatica is species of turmeric native to Western Ghats. It has fragrant cream coloured rhizomes. These rhizomes are often used in cosmetics in South India.

It is called "Jungli haldi" in hindi , "Ambe Halad" in marathi and "Kasthuri Manjal" in malayalam.

It has medicinal importance in chinese medicinal methods for curing cancer. It also has anti-biotic properties. It has aromatic volatile oils that help to reduce inflammation and excessive lipids from the blood.

It grows vigorously in monsoon and has beautiful pinkish white flowers with orange tinge. These flowers are often used in decorating houses. These flowers remain fresh for about 10 days. Villagers often use their Rhizomes to add flavour to their curries.
