Is it a tail-less bulbul ? Let's find out.

Lots of birds have the ability to lose their tail to escape from predators like seen in lizards. This is called "Fright moult" or "Shock moult".

Birds confuse their predators either by leaving it with a mouth full of feathers or by leaving a puff of feathers behind. The bird will be tail-less for 4 - 6 weeks until new feathers grow in.

In the following video , you will see a tail-less red whiskered bulbul who might have experienced a fright moult. We have captured its journey from no-tail to a somewhat tail growing back.
